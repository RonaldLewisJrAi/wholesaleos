# WholesaleOS Knowledge Base

## 1. Platform Overview

WholesaleOS operates as a multi-sided real estate transaction network functioning as a Bloomberg Terminal for real estate transactions.

It connects five personas:

Wholesalers
Investors
Realtors
Title Companies
Super Admin

Ecosystem Workflow

Wholesaler sources property
Deal Analyzer evaluates opportunity
Deal published to Deal Room
Investor reserves the deal
Title Company verifies escrow
Tri-Party Verification confirms closing
Trust Scores update automatically

## 5. Deal Intelligence

Deal Score (0-100)

Calculated from:

• Equity Spread
• Buyer Demand
• Rehab Risk
• Seller Motivation
• Wholesaler Trust Score

Liquidity Signal
Predicts deal velocity based on 90-day cash buyer activity.

Risk Level
Detects flips with tight margins or high rehab risk.

Equity Spread
Difference between ARV and total capital required.

Recommended Offer
Maximum Allowable Offer calculated using the 70% rule.

Lead Intake
Deal Analysis
Publishing

Deal Status Definitions

DRAFT
Deal is still being analyzed.

ACTIVE
Visible in the marketplace.

RESERVED
Investor deposit placed.

UNDER ESCROW
Title company confirmed EMD.

CLOSED
Tri-Party Verification completed.

Triple Verification System

A deal is recorded as Verified Closed only when:

Wholesaler confirms assignment execution
Investor confirms purchase completion
Title Company confirms escrow closure

This prevents fake deals and automatically increases Trust Scores.

## 7. Deal Publishing Requirements

A deal must meet these requirements before publishing:

• Minimum of 3 property photos
• Repair estimate entered
• ARV calculated
• Proof of Control uploaded
• Status set to ACTIVE

Deals missing these remain in Draft.
